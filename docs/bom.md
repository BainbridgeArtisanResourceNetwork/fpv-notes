ETA Drones 102 - July 2022 class

Components suggested are:

  * DJI V2 FPV Goggles
  * CaddxFPV Air Unit Micro, Digital VTX, and Camera
  * HGLRC Sector5 V3 HD Freestyle Frame Kit:
  * HGLRC 7-inch Motor Arms (4)
  * T-MOTOR P2505 1850KV MOTORS (4-5)
  * HGLRC ZEUS F748 FC STACK 
  * ETHIX P3 PEANUT BUTTER AND JELLY PROPS 2-SETS of 4
  * TBS Crossfire NANO RX PRO Long Range Drone Receiver
  * TBS CROSSFIRE MICRO TX V2 TX MODULE
  * Radio Master TX16S Mark II V4.0 Hall Gimbals Transmitter
  * Matek M8Q-8883 GPS Module

Some optional components to consider:

  * Lumenier AXII HD 2 Patch Visor 5.8GHz Antenna Combo Set for DJI FPV Goggles
  * VIFLY Finder 2 Drone Buzzer

You will also need a charger:

  * iSDT 608AC Smart Battery Charger AC 50W DC 200W 8A w/ Detachable Power Supply; or
  * ISDT K2 200/500W 20A 2-6S AC/DC Dual Channel AC Smart Charger

For batteries (make sure all use XT60 plug):

  * ZOHD Lionpack 18650 4S1P 3500mAh 14.8V Li-ion Battery (starter pack);
  * Auline Li-Ion SONY VTC5A 6S 2600MAH 22.2V 35A XT60 (long-range pack);
  * Tattu R-Line Version 4.0 1550mAh 22.2V 130C 6S1P Lipo Battery Pack XT60 (high-performance pack).
